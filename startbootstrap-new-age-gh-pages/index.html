TastyWorld

TastyWorld is an application focused on making recipes available all over the world.

Voice Assistant

Play pre-recorded audio with the entire step-by-step process of executing the recipe, without needing to consult your device while preparing the recipe

AR View

From now on you can see your recipes in augmented reality!

Recipe Printing

You can now print your recipes through TastyWorld

Recipe Sharing

If you really like our recipe, you can share it with whoever you want

Description

TastyWorld is an app that connects cultures through recipes, making global flavors accessible and inspiring food lovers to explore the world's culinary diversity.
